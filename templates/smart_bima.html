Why Crop Insurance?

  • Protection against natural disasters
  • Financial security for crop loss
  • Government subsidy benefits
  • Peace of mind during cultivation
  • Quick claim settlement process

What's Covered?

Natural Calamities:

Drought, flood, hailstorm, cyclone

Pest & Disease:

Insect attacks, crop diseases

Post-Harvest:

Losses during drying (up to 14 days)

Localized Risks:

Landslide, fire, lightning

Insurance Tips

  • ✓ Apply before crop sowing deadline
  • ✓ Keep land records ready
  • ✓ Declare correct sum insured
  • ✓ Report crop loss within 72 hours
  • ✓ Maintain proper documentation
  • ✓ Use government subsidy schemes
